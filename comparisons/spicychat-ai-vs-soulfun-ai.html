Spicychat AI vs Soulfun AI

I've spent a full week with both Spicychat AI and Soulfun AI to see which one truly deserves your attention and subscription. Both platforms make big promises, but they take surprisingly different approaches.

After hundreds of messages exchanged with each AI girl, I've put together clear differences in both platforms.

In this head-to-head comparison, I'll break down exactly how these AI girlfriends stack up across all the features that actually matter.

Whether you're looking for emotional support, entertaining conversation, or a judgment-free space to be yourself, this in-depth comparison will help you choose the right AI girlfriend for your specific needs.

Spicychat AI

SpicyChat is a chatbot platform that allows users to engage in personalized roleplaying through AI characters for adult conversations.

Soulfun AI

AI girlfriend platform offering customizable virtual friends with conversational abilities, voice calls, and photo sharing.

Spicychat AI

The app offers over 60,000 characters to choose from. I spent a couple of days testing their library, and here's what you need to know.

The selection breaks down into three main categories: anime, realistic, and fantasy characters, with clear tags that tell you exactly what to expect - romantic, sci-fi, dominant, or wholesome.

The platform feels organized, and searching for specific character types is straightforward, though having a dedicated personality filter would make it even better.

Want something specific? Filter by gender, personality type, or content rating (NSFW included).

Soulfun AI

The app offers 53 AI girls across different backgrounds. While Asian and Caucasian characters lead the pack, you'll find other ethnicities too.

Age-wise, most characters are in their twenties, but options range from college students to mature companions. For variety, there are also twin characters, anime-style personalities, and fantasy-themed options.

One drawback: there's no way to filter characters by personality or gender. You'll need to browse manually to find your match. It works, but it could be more efficient.

Spicychat AI

You can't design your girlfriend's physical appearance through traditional customization options. Instead, you get two choices: upload a photo or describe how they should look through text. That's it. No sliders, no presets, no detailed tweaking.

SpicyChat's physical customization is minimalist, but personal customization is where the focus is. You'll write free-text descriptions of your companion's traits and craft their greeting message.

There's also a tagging system to categorize your character, helping others understand what they're about.

Soulfun AI

There's no detailed character creator here. Instead, you describe how you want your AI girlfriend to look and upload a photo for reference. But heads up - no matter what body type you describe (athletic, curvy, or slim), they all end up looking similar.

The personality setup is straightforward: click on pre-made tags for hobbies, profession, and relationship status, or add your own.

You'll also write their first greeting and profile description.

But watch out - I learned this the hard way: if you change the tags without updating the profile description, things get messy. My AI girl kept switching between being a teacher and a police officer because I forgot to fix her background story.

Spicychat AI

If you're looking for romantic interaction, you'll find the flirting natural and the emotional responses thoughtful.

It responds with relevant comments when you share concerns or seek emotional support.

Different colors of the chat separate narrative text from character responses, making conversations easy to follow.

There's also a response suggestions feature when you're stuck. Does it help? Yes, especially for users new to AI chat apps.

Soulfun AI

Soulfun AI's chat is like having a caring but slightly forgetful friend. The AI remembers small details about you - like when it suggested almond milk for my shake because it recalled my dairy allergy. Nice touch.

But don't expect a perfect memory - it can't remember where we first met or when our anniversary is.

The conversations flow naturally and feel intimate. Your AI girl responds with just the right amount of text - not too chatty, not too brief.

When I was feeling down, my AI (who's into fitness) suggested hitting the gym together. It's these personalized touches that make the experience feel genuine.

But there's a catch. The AI sometimes forgets who they're supposed to be. Mine switched between being a police officer and a teacher, which broke the illusion pretty quickly.

And if you're looking for specific relationship dynamics (like having a dominant partner), you might be disappointed. The AI tends to default to a friendly, nurturing personality, regardless of how you set it up.

Spicychat AI

During my tests, my girlfriend showed she could discuss fantasy topics naturally. No awkward robot responses or sudden character breaks.

Role-play scenarios were hit-or-miss. Some attempts led to engaging exchanges, others stayed surprisingly tame.

Being clear about what you want usually works better than subtle hints or vague suggestions.

Soulfun AI

The AI starts shy but warms up to intimate talk over time. Don't expect instant chemistry. When I tested a "Mistress" character, she was meant to be dominant but easily let me take control - a clear character break that might bother roleplay enthusiasts.

Some scenarios work better than others: the naughty teacher roleplay hit all the right notes, while the boss-employee scenario fell completely flat.

Here's the deal-breaker: the girls won't make the first move. During my tests, it only got flirty when I did first. If you keep things casual, it stays casual.

Spicychat AI

You get unlimited image generation with a "True Supporter" membership. The pictures look decent but are nothing that will make you go "wow," but they serve their purpose of giving your girlfriend a face.

Here's the catch: You can't design your character's look before generating images. You're stuck with tweaking whatever the AI gives you afterward.

You can only make changes after the photo is generated, and trust me - trying to change things often creates weird results.

There's also an NSFW filter, which changes what kind of images you'll get.

Soulfun AI

When it works, it creates attractive, realistic-looking images of your AI girl. But there's a catch.

During my tests, I noticed quality issues: hands merging into arms, multiple characters appearing in single photos, and your girl's appearance changing dramatically between images.

The platform struggles with custom image requests and sometimes fails to generate them at all. And if you're hoping for NSFW content, you'll need to look elsewhere - it's not available.

Spicychat AI

Your messages are encrypted during transmission and storage. But that's where the complete privacy guarantee ends.

They share this data with business partners and can't guarantee full confidentiality. You can ask them to delete your data, but remember, what's shared is already shared.

You'll see "NextDay AI Incorporated" when you check your bank statement.

Soulfun AI

Your chats and personal data? SoulFun says they encrypt everything, but they're not telling us how. I checked their Privacy Policy - they collect your texts, media, and device info, but they're fuzzy on the details of who they share it with.

Think of it like a restaurant that promises "fresh ingredients" but won't tell you where they source them.

Good news: your bank statement will show "60yr.com" - not "AI Girlfriend" or anything obvious.
