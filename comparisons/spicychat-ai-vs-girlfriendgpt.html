Spicychat AI

The app offers over 60,000 characters to choose from. I spent a couple of days testing their library, and here's what you need to know.

The selection breaks down into three main categories: anime, realistic, and fantasy characters, with clear tags that tell you exactly what to expect - romantic, sci-fi, dominant, or wholesome.

The platform feels organized, and searching for specific character types is straightforward, though having a dedicated personality filter would make it even better.

Want something specific? Filter by gender, personality type, or content rating (NSFW included).

GirlfriendGPT

The app offers thousands of characters across anime, fantasy, realistic, and scenario-based styles. I tested their character selection system extensively, and here's what you need to know.

Whether you want a dominant personality, a fantasy creature, or a realistic companion, the search system helps you find matches quickly. The platform separates SFW and NSFW characters clearly, so you always know what to expect.

Most characters come from the user community, which means new options appear daily. You can sort by popularity, likes, or newest additions to find trending companions.

Spicychat AI

You can't design your girlfriend's physical appearance through traditional customization options. Instead, you get two choices: upload a photo or describe how they should look through text. That's it. No sliders, no presets, no detailed tweaking.

SpicyChat's physical customization is minimalist, but personal customization is where the focus is. You'll write free-text descriptions of your companion's traits and craft their greeting message.

There's also a tagging system to categorize your character, helping others understand what they're about.

GirlfriendGPT

The basics are straightforward: upload a photo or describe how you want your girlfriend to look.

You can add tags to highlight her personality and interests so others can better understand what she's all about.

If you want detailed appearance controls, you'll find the options limited. But if you're more interested in crafting personality and backstory, you'll find plenty of room for creativity with a text-base editor.

She stays in character, whether you're having casual banter or deeper conversations. Nothing feels forced or robotic.

What sets it apart is the built-in suggestion system. Think of it as subtle prompts that keep conversations flowing naturally.

Spicychat AI

You get unlimited image generation with a "True Supporter" membership. The pictures look decent but are nothing that will make you go "wow," but they serve their purpose of giving your girlfriend a face.

Here's the catch: You can't design your character's look before generating images. You're stuck with tweaking whatever the AI gives you afterward.

You can only make changes after the photo is generated, and trust me - trying to change things often creates weird results.

There's also an NSFW filter, which changes what kind of images you'll get.

GirlfriendGPT

The images are sharp, high-resolution, and genuinely attractive. But each generated image shows a slightly different girl. While they're all attractive, this inconsistency breaks the illusion of chatting with the same person.

Please note that the platform tends to default to NSFW content, even with seemingly innocent prompts, which can result in unexpected poses or nude images.

The pricing structure doesn't help, either. Want to see pictures directly in your chat? With the Premium plan, you can create images but only view them in a separate gallery.

If you want to see pictures directly in your chat, you'll need the Deluxe or Elite plan.
