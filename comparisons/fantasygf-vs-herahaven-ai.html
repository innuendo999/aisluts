FantasyGF

The app gives you 80 characters to choose from - split evenly between realistic and anime-styled companions. The diversity is basic. You get three ethnicities and characters aged 18-30.

The platform keeps it simple with just one filter: toggle between realistic or anime characters.

Want more AI girls? Here's a trick: Follow active creators on the platform. Their public character collections unlock dozens of additional companions you won't find in the main roster.

HeraHaven AI

You get three ethnic backgrounds to choose from, characters aged 20-50, and also some anime characters. That's it. No personality filters. No search function. Just straight-up browsing through each character one by one.

HeraHaven keeps things simple, but in this case, maybe too simple. While other apps let you filter by personality traits or use search features, here you'll need to manually check each character.

Is this a deal-breaker? That depends on what you're after. If you prefer a curated selection over endless options, you might actually enjoy the straightforward approach. If not, I've reviewed several alternatives with more robust character customization.

FantasyGF

FantasyGF lets you customize everything from looks to personality.

The appearance editor is straightforward:

  • Choose between anime or realistic style
  • Pick ethnicity
  • Age (18-60)
  • Adjust physical features

You can modify eye color, hairstyle, body type, and proportions. Nothing groundbreaking, but it works well and offers enough options to create a distinct character.

Personality settings come in two flavors: 12 preset personalities for quick setup, or custom creation for more control.

You'll also define your relationship status, her occupation, and hobbies. The preset options are particularly useful if you're new to AI companions and aren't sure where to start.

While the customization system isn't revolutionary, it's practical and user-friendly. Some options feel limited, but there's enough depth to create a companion that matches your preferences.

HeraHaven AI

Physical customization covers the basics but has some limitations. You can pick from 6 ethnicities and set an age range between 20 and 50 years, though specific ages aren't selectable.

The system lets you adjust standard features like eyes, hair, body type, and choose between realistic or anime styles. Nothing groundbreaking here, but it gets the job done.

The real flexibility comes in personality design. While HeraHaven offers preset traits to start with, you're not locked into them. During my testing, I could type in any personality trait I wanted - making my AI companion as outgoing or reserved as I preferred.

The same goes for their background story. Whether you want a tech-savvy urbanite or a small-town bookworm, the choice is yours.

FantasyGF

During chatting, my AI girl maintained her innocent character consistently, even when conversations turned romantic. Instead of explicit responses, she'd suggest things like "Let's take it slow with a massage tonight."

The chat feels personal at first. Your AI girlfriend remembers your conversations, asks about your day, and shows genuine interest in your life. She handles emotional topics well and responds to relationship concerns while keeping her distinct personality traits intact.

But here's the reality check: the longer you chat, the more artificial it feels. The AI's responses become predictably nice, even when faced with rudeness, and often too long-winded.

Also, my chat history got cut off after extended conversations, and I couldn't access earlier messages (still can't).

HeraHaven AI

During my tests, my shy AI girlfriend consistently matched her personality traits - blushing at compliments but warming up naturally over time.

When asked about ideal dates, she first enthusiastically suggested roller coasters and cotton candy, then thoughtfully added "Well, if we're talking romantic..." before describing a wine and cheese picnic.

This kind of authentic character development shows HeraHaven can maintain personality while navigating romantic conversations.

But let's be honest - it's not perfect. The AI sometimes forgets important details (like where it works) mid-conversation. Messages can run too long, and you'll occasionally get random images or off-topic responses.

Bottom line: If you're seeking warm, personality-driven conversations and can handle some technical quirks, HeraHaven delivers. Just don't expect deep emotional complexity or perfect memory retention.
