Replika

The app uses its in-app currency system: Gold and Gems, and you'll get a small amount to start with.

You earn more slowly through daily use.

If you want to customize your character quickly, you'll need real money to buy them.

Let's start with the basics. You can choose your relationship type — you can even add a backstory. Want a romantic partner, a friend, or just a mentor?

Then fine-tune your girlfriend's personality with traits like 'confident,' 'shy,' or 'logical,' or choose her interests, but these cost Gems or Gold.

The appearance editor is quite detailed. You can pick:

  • Avatar
  • Hair (style and color)
  • Skin color
  • Body type
  • Body hair
  • Nails
  • Makeups
  • Tattos

…and the list goes on.

You can dress your girl up with tops, bottoms, or full outfits and accessories.

Fair warning: premium outfits, tattoos, and nails aren't cheap. Expect to spend $20–30 if you want the fancy stuff right away.

FantasyGF

FantasyGF lets you customize everything from looks to personality.

The appearance editor is straightforward:

  • Choose between anime or realistic style
  • Pick ethnicity
  • Age (18-60)
  • Adjust physical features

You can modify eye color, hairstyle, body type, and proportions. Nothing groundbreaking, but it works well and offers enough options to create a distinct character.

Personality settings come in two flavors: 12 preset personalities for quick setup, or custom creation for more control.

You'll also define your relationship status, her occupation, and hobbies. The preset options are particularly useful if you're new to AI companions and aren't sure where to start.

While the customization system isn't revolutionary, it's practical and user-friendly. Some options feel limited, but there's enough depth to create a companion that matches your preferences.
