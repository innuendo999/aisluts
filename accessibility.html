Accessibility

At Top AI Girlfriend, we are dedicated to enhancing the user experience for everyone. As technology advances, we continue to learn and grow, making accessibility a core focus in our software development process. We highly value feedback and are eager to hear from you on how we can further improve our products.

In line with our commitment, we regularly evaluate our products using assistive technologies, such as screen reading software. We have fully embraced digital inclusion by adhering to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) V2.1 Level AA in our design and development efforts.

Assistive Technology

The Top AI Girlfriend platform is thoroughly tested across various browsers, screen sizes, and is compatible with screen readers. We recommend using the following browsers (current and previous major releases):

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

For the best experience with screen readers, we recommend:

  • Windows: NVDA
  • MacOS: VoiceOver

Reporting Accessibility Issues

If you encounter any accessibility issues with our products, please reach out to us via email: elena@topaigirlfriend.com.
